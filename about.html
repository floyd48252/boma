About Boma Mabati Factory

Your Trusted Partner for Quality Iron Sheets

Who We Are

Boma Mabati Factory is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality iron sheets in Nairobi. Located at the heart of Ruiru Bypass, we have been serving construction contractors, builders, and individual customers with premium iron sheet products at competitive prices.

Our factory is equipped with modern machinery and skilled workforce, ensuring every product meets international quality standards while maintaining affordable pricing.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide affordable quality materials to the construction industry, Boma Mabati Factory has grown to become a trusted name in Nairobi. We started with a small operation and through dedication to quality and customer service, we have expanded our product range and customer base significantly.

Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers ranging from individual homebuilders to large construction firms. Our success is built on the foundation of trust, reliability, and competitive pricing.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality iron sheets at affordable prices, ensuring every customer can access premium building materials without breaking the bank.

Our Vision

To be the leading iron sheet manufacturer in East Africa, recognized for quality, affordability, and excellent customer service.
